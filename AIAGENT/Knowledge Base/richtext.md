## What Happy People AI Does

Happy People AI specialises in end-to-end GoHighLevel implementation and ongoing support. They turn GHL from a complex platform into a business's most powerful growth tool. Services span nine core areas:

### 1. CRM Setup & Optimization
Full GoHighLevel CRM configuration tailored to the client's pipeline, deal stages, and team workflow. Includes contact segmentation, custom fields, pipeline design, and automation triggers so no lead ever falls through the cracks.

### 2. Workflow Automation
Building powerful automated workflows inside GHL — lead nurturing sequences, follow-up campaigns, appointment reminders, internal task assignments. Reduces manual work so the team can focus on closing deals, not chasing them.

### 3. AI Chatbot Integration
Deploying AI-powered chatbots on websites, Facebook, Instagram, and SMS. Bots qualify leads 24/7, answer FAQs, collect contact details, and push warm prospects directly into the CRM — without any human intervention.

### 4. Funnel & Website Design
High-converting landing pages, sales funnels, and full websites built inside GHL. Every page is optimised for speed, mobile responsiveness, and conversion — backed by copywriting and design best practices.

### 5. Email & SMS Marketing
Complete email and SMS campaign setup: templates, broadcast scheduling, automated drip sequences, list segmentation, and performance tracking. Helps clients stay top-of-mind and drive repeat business.

### 6. Reputation Management
Automated review request campaigns that turn happy customers into 5-star reviews on Google and other platforms. Includes negative feedback routing and review monitoring dashboards.

### 7. Reporting & Analytics
Custom dashboards inside GHL that surface the KPIs that actually matter — leads generated, conversion rates, revenue attributed, campaign ROI. Makes data-driven decisions easy.

### 8. White-Label GHL Setup
For marketing agencies wanting to resell GHL under their own brand. Happy People AI handles the full white-label configuration — custom domain, branding, client sub-account structure, and onboarding templates.

### 9. Ongoing Support & Training
Monthly retainer plans covering platform updates, new automation builds, staff training, and dedicated support. Clients are never left to figure things out alone.
